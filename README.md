# SQL Portfolio: Business Analytics Scenarios

A collection of SQL analyses addressing real-world business questions across marketing, operations, and customer service using Brazilian e-commerce data.

## Overview

This project demonstrates how to translate business requests into actionable SQL queries and insights. Each scenario reflects the kind of ad hoc analysis a data analyst handles regularly—from evaluating seller performance to measuring campaign effectiveness.

## Dataset

**Source:** [Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/olistbr/brazilian-ecommerce/data)

The dataset includes order details, customer information, seller data, product categories, and payment records from a Brazilian e-commerce platform.

## Content

### Scenario 1: Seller Delivery Performance
Identifies the fastest sellers by average delivery time over the last 3 months, helping operations understand what top performers do well.

### Scenario 2: Late Delivery Analysis  
Investigates whether São Paulo genuinely has a late delivery problem compared to other major cities, providing customer service with data-driven context.

### Scenario 3: Top High-Value Customers
Finds the biggest spenders in Q1 2018 and their geographic distribution, giving marketing clear targets for retention campaigns.

### Scenario 4: City Sales Rankings
Ranks cities by total revenue in 2017 to help regional sales focus efforts where demand is strongest.

### Scenario 5: Discount Campaign Impact
Measures whether a week-long electronics discount campaign increased orders and revenue, comparing results before, during, and after the promotion.
